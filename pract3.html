Priyadarshini J.L. College of Engineering is a flagship institution belonging to Lokmanya Tilak Jankalyan Shikshan Sanstha established in the year 2008. The college was located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. PJLCE is an autonomous institute affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University and accredited with NAAC Grade A. PJLCE Nagpur offers UG courses in Engineering with specialisations including Computer Science Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Civil Engineering and Electrical Engineering. B.E. / B.Tech is among the popular flagship courses. The institute offers this program in full-time mode. Priyadarshini J.L. College of Engineering admission is based on entrance exams. The college accepts national entrance exams such as JEE Main, MHT CET, and others. The college has well-equipped infrastructure which includes classrooms, an auditorium, a conference hall, a library, a sports complex, a hostel, and others.
